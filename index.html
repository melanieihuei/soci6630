About

All contents are related to the text book An R companion to applied regression by John Fox and Sanford Weisberg. The official website of the book is here. The only purpose of this website is to provide R scripts and exercises used in course SOCI6630. Any kind of commercial purposes is not allowed.

This is not an official website of SOCI 6630, every announcement are based on the eLC.
